Broiled Salmon on Rice With Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 head broccoli, florets only, chopped (about 2 cups)
  • 4 pieces skinless salmon fillet (1¼ pounds total)
  • 1 large red onion, cut into ¼ inch-thick wedges
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Kosher salt and black pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat the broiler. In a small bowl, combine sugar and soy sauce, and then set aside.
  2. Cook rice according to package directions, and then stir in broccoli during the last 3 minutes.
  3. Remove the rice and broccoli from heat, let stand until the broccoli is tender, about 5 minutes, and then fluff with a fork.
  4. Meanwhile, place the salmon and onion on a rimmed baking sheet, drizzle with oil, and season with ½ teaspoon salt and ¼ teaspoon pepper.
  5. Broil until the salmon is opaque throughout, 8 to 10 minutes. During the last 2 minutes of cooking, spoon half the soy sauce glaze over the fish.
  6. Serve the salmon, onion, and broccoli-rice mixture with the remaining glaze.
